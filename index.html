About Me

I’m Rajeswari Anand, a passionate and multi-faceted student with a deep interest in computer science, artificial intelligence, and robotics. Having grown up in Dubai, I’ve always embraced a global perspective while staying strongly rooted in my Indian culture. My academic journey reflects a balance of excellence and curiosity — from scoring a centum in Mathematics in the ICSE board exams to exploring AI through internships and virtual experience programs with leading companies. Beyond academics, I take pride in leadership roles in my school’s Student Welfare Association and Student Council, consistently striving to make a positive impact on those around me. I believe in continuous growth, collaboration, and embracing challenges with a positive outlook.

Hobbies

Tennis is my go-to outlet for physical energy and mental sharpness. The sport has taught me the importance of focus, resilience, and strategic thinking. Whether I'm in a competitive match or simply practicing, I enjoy the rhythm and intensity it brings, and I value how it strengthens both my body and mind.

Bharatanatyam has been an integral part of my life for several years. As a professionally trained dancer, I’ve learned not only the grace and discipline of this classical Indian art form but also its deeper cultural and spiritual significance. Performing on stage has helped me express emotions beyond words and instilled in me confidence, patience, and a strong sense of tradition.

Reading is my quiet escape. I enjoy exploring a variety of genres — from contemporary fiction and poetry to articles on science and innovation. Books help me unwind, spark my imagination, and fuel my curiosity about the world around me.

Music has always been a source of comfort and creativity for me. I’m especially drawn to indie music because of its raw emotion and unique storytelling. The melodies and lyrics often resonate with my mood and thoughts, making music a constant companion in my day-to-day life.

Achievements

In 2021, CF Tennis Academy selected a handful of players globally and honored them as the Best Tennis Player of the Year. I am proud to be one of those athletes.

Being elected as the Deputy Head of the Student Welfare Association was a proud and meaningful milestone in my school journey. I worked closely with my peers and teachers to address student concerns, organize initiatives, and promote a positive environment.

Receiving the JSS Scholar Award for academic excellence was a proud milestone in my academic journey. It recognized my consistent performance, especially in subjects like Mathematics and Computer Science, and motivated me to continue striving for excellence.

Qualifying as a professional Bharatanatyam dancer after completing seven levels of training has been a proud cultural and personal achievement, reflecting years of dedication and discipline.
